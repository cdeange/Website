Welcome.

Let's start from the beginning.

I was raised in a sleepy Canadian town called Bolton. Not much to see, not much to do. I have since crawled out of the crib and found myself studying Software Engineering at University of Waterloo. Another small town, filled with students. It's a great school with a great co-op program.

I was a very curious child, so I naturally developed a variety of hobbies throughout the years. I've been a coin-collecting enthusiast for 15 years. I practiced Shōtōkan karate for 6 years, attaining a rank of black belt. Throughout highschool, I developed many 2D animations using Adobe Flash, for personal, academic, and competitive presentations. I am an avid skier, although recently I've taken up skiboarding.

The journey of a thousand bugs begins with a single boolean.

After enjoying the CS courses I took throughout highschool, I realized that writing software was meant to be my livelihood. My primary focus is on Android development. Here are some recent projects of mine:

CareZone

My most memorable co-op experience was working at CareZone. Their services provide the resources and tools needed to make taking care of your loved ones an easier and more organized experience.

GitHub Status

I build this out of necessity after finding I was checking the GitHub status page. It is planned to have push notifications incorporated into the app.

Quicklink

Generate shortlinks to your favourite sites. My first experimentation with the Android sharing platform, networking, and persistent storage through a local SQLite database.

AutoTalk

My first side project, originally intended for my own use. Uses text-to-speech and speech-to-text to dictate SMS messages and allow you to respond, giving you a hands-free experience.
